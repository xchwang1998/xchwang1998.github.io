About me

I am pursuing the M.S. degree in photogrammetry and remote sensing from Wuhan University, Wuhan, China (expected in June 2022). I work in the field of Airborne lidar data post-processing and 3D lidar SLAM. My advisor is Prof. Hongchao Ma and Prof. Xinlian Liang. Before that, I received the B.S. degree in Surveying and Mapping Engineering from Northeastern University, Shenyang, China in June 2019.

Research

My research interests include

  • Airborne lidar data post-processing

  • Landcover classification

  • 3D lidar SLAM

  • Machine Learning
